About


Adidas has released a new shoe to celebrate 40 years of Terry Fox's marathon of hope. Introducing the Terry Tracker!

If you want a shoe to keep you moving or want a quality shoe that's not overpriced, you have to try the Terry Tracker.

This smart shoe connects to a Terry Tracker app and tracks the amount of km and steps you've walked. $5 will be donated to cancer research every time you walk the distance it took for Terry to walk to one of his stops.

This new and improved model of the shoe that allowed Terry Fox to run 5373 km can now be yours. For $200 dollars only.
